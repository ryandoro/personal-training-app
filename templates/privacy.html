Privacy Policy

At FitBaseAI, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information you provide directly, including your name, email, age, weight, height, exercise history, fitness goals, and any other information submitted in your training questionnaire. We may also collect payment details when you subscribe through our payment provider (Stripe).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to generate personalized workouts, track your progress, manage your subscription, and improve our services. We may also send you important updates related to your account.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We use secure methods to store and transmit your data. While no system is 100% secure, we take reasonable steps to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted third-party providers, such as Stripe (for payment processing) and Postmark (for email delivery), strictly for service-related purposes.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at hello@fitbaseai.com.

6. Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

By using FitBaseAI, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
